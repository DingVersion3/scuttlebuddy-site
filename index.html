What it does

Every command, grouped by what your server actually asks for.

Register your Riot account once, and ScuttleBuddy keeps everything else running in the background — syncing match history, watching for new games, and posting to your leaderboard channel automatically.

Track & Compare

  • /registerLink your Discord account to your Riot ID and region.
  • /lastgameFull recap of your (or anyone's) most recent match.
  • /leaderboardServer rankings by win rate, KDA, rank, and more.
  • /compareHead-to-head weekly stats between two players.
  • /nemesisThe enemy champion that beats you the most.
  • /duoWin rate when two specific players queue together.
  • /whoshouldiplayChampion suggestions from your own win-rate data.

Live Alerts

  • /streakCheck anyone's current win or loss streak.
  • Auto-alertsPosts on win/loss streaks, rank changes, and standout games — no command needed.

Honeyfruit Betting

  • /openbetOpen betting on your own next game.
  • /betWager Honeyfruit on someone's open game.
  • /honeyfruitCheck your (fake currency) balance.
  • /dailybonusClaim a daily Honeyfruit top-up.

Fun & Admin

  • /randomchampRandom champion with a fully legal rune page.
  • /memestatsWeekly superlatives — most deaths, best KDA, and more.
  • /teamcompRandom champion & position assignment for your lobby.
  • /setleaderboardchannelChoose where weekly posts show up (admin).
